Britton & Hussain On Tour

As of September 2020, myself and my dear friend Saarah are in honours year of Finance study at the University of Strathclyde (as you will already know because you've read every page on this site in the logical horizontal sequence presented on the primary navigation bar). This blog entry will follow our latest café tour around Giffnock, Newton Mearns, Shawlands, Muirend, Queen's Park and the CBD. The aim of this café tour is to allow space, mental clarity, collusion and impeccable company throughout the dissertation writing process. And so forth, begin, develop and consolidate our dissertations. But let's not forget those crucial coffee ratings! (ranking system pending) Expect new posts every Tuesday, after our visits. Brought to you alongside special guest: Saarah Hussain.

The Gentlemen Who Café

This blog entry explores a perpetual, life-long café tour around the UK. The stops on this tour include various department store, supermarket, garden centre, furniture store and stand-alone cafés. They will be rated on a '0-M&S' scale with the highest score being 'M&S'. The rating will be displayed on a metaphorical basis involving an M&S scenario. Brought to you alongside special guest: Creg "Vi" Griffin.
